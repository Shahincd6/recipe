Homemade French Fries Recipe

French fries are a beloved classic, known for their crispy exterior
and fluffy interior. Making them at home is easier than you might think!

French Fries Ingredients:

  • 4 large russet potatoes
  • Vegetable oil (for frying)
  • Salt, to taste

How to make French Fries:

  1. Prep the Potatoes:
    • Begin by peeling the potatoes. You can leave the skin on for a more rustic texture if you prefer.
    • Cut the potatoes into even-sized matchsticks or strips. Try to make them as uniform in size as possible to ensure even cooking.
  2. Rinse and Soak:
    • Place the potato strips in a large bowl of cold water. This helps remove excess starch and prevents the fries from sticking together during frying.
    • Allow the potatoes to soak for at least 30 minutes, or up to a few hours for best results.
  3. Drain and Dry:
    • Drain the soaked potatoes and pat them dry thoroughly using a kitchen towel or paper towels. Removing excess moisture is crucial for crispy fries.
  4. Preheat the Oil:
    • Pour vegetable oil into a deep, heavy-bottomed pot or a deep fryer. You'll need enough oil to submerge the fries completely. Heat the oil to 350-375°F (175-190°C).
  5. Fry the Fries:
    • Carefully add a handful of the dried potato strips to the hot oil. Be cautious to avoid overcrowding the pot, as it can cause the temperature to drop and result in soggy fries.
    • Fry the potatoes in batches for about 3-4 minutes or until they turn golden brown and crispy. Use a slotted spoon or a wire mesh skimmer to remove the fries from the oil and transfer them to a paper towel-lined plate to drain excess oil.
    • Repeat this process with the remaining batches of potatoes.
  6. Season and Serve:
    • While the fries are still hot, season them generously with salt. You can also experiment with other seasonings like garlic powder, paprika, or cayenne pepper for added flavor.
    • Serve the hot and crispy homemade French fries immediately with your favorite dipping sauces, such as ketchup, mayo, or aioli.

Enjoy your delicious homemade French fries!
They're perfect as a snack or a side dish for burgers, sandwiches, or other favorite meals.
