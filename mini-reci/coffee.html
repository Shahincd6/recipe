Cold Coffee Recipe

Homemade cold coffee is a delightful and refreshing beverage, perfect for those warm summer days.

Cold Coffee Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of brewed coffee (strongly brewed and cooled to room temperature)
  • 2 cups of milk (whole milk or your preferred milk)
  • 1/4 cup of granulated sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 1/4 cup of water
  • 1/4 cup of heavy cream (optional for a richer texture)
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • Ice cubes
  • Whipped cream (optional, for garnish)
  • Chocolate syrup or cocoa powder (optional, for garnish)

How to make Cold Coffee:

  1. Prepare the Coffee:
    • Brew a strong cup of coffee using your preferred method. You can use a drip coffee maker, French press, or instant coffee.
    • Allow the brewed coffee to cool to room temperature. You can speed up the cooling process by placing it in the refrigerator for a while.
  2. Prepare Simple Syrup:
    • In a small saucepan, combine the granulated sugar and water.
    • Heat the mixture over low heat, stirring constantly until the sugar completely dissolves.
    • Remove the syrup from heat and let it cool. This syrup will sweeten your cold coffee without the need for granulated sugar, which can be challenging to dissolve in cold liquid.
  3. Prepare the Cold Coffee:
    • Fill your serving glasses with ice cubes. You can use regular ice cubes or coffee ice cubes for an extra coffee kick.
    • Pour the cold coffee mixture over the ice cubes, filling each glass about two-thirds full.
  4. Garnish and Serve:
    • If desired, top each glass of cold coffee with a dollop of whipped cream.
    • Drizzle some chocolate syrup or dust with cocoa powder on top for an added touch of flavor and presentation.
  5. Enjoy:
    • Stir the cold coffee gently before sipping, and enjoy your homemade cold coffee.

Feel free to customize your cold coffee by adding a splash of flavored syrups, a scoop of ice cream,
or a dusting of cinnamon for extra flavor variations.
