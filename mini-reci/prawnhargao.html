Prawn Har gao

Prawn Har Gao, also known as Ha Gao or Xia Jiao, is a delicious Cantonese dim sum dish consisting of translucent dumplings filled with succulent shrimp. Here's a recipe for making Prawn Har Gao at home:

Prawn Har Gao Ingredients

These are the basic ingredients you’ll need to make this Prawn Har Gao recipe at home:

For Dumbling Wrapper

  • 1 cup wheat starch
  • 2 tablespoons tapioca starch
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup boiling water
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil

For Fillings

  • 1/2 pound fresh shrimp, peeled, deveined, and finely chopped
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon white pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon cornstarch
  • 1 teaspoon minced ginger
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 1 green onion, finely chopped

How to make Prawn Har Gao

You’ll find the full, step-by-step recipe below — but here’s a brief overview of
what you can expect when you make homemade Prawn Har Gao at home:

  1. Make the dumpling wrapper: Combine wheat starch, tapioca starch, and salt. Gradually add boiling water, then vegetable oil. Knead into a smooth dough.
  2. Prepare the filling: Mix shrimp with salt, sugar, white pepper, sesame oil, cornstarch, ginger, garlic, and green onion.
  3. Divide dough into small balls, then flatten one into a 3-inch circle.
  4. Place a spoonful of shrimp filling in the center, fold in half, and seal edges with pleats.
  5. Steam dumplings for 5-6 minutes until translucent and filling is cooked.
  6. Serve hot with a soy sauce, rice vinegar, and chili sauce dipping mixture if desired. Enjoy your Prawn HarGao!

Tip from recipe creator: "Keep the dumpling wrappers covered with a damp cloth to prevent drying out while assembling your Har Gao.
