Tom Yum Soup-Prawns

Tom Yum Soup is a popular and iconic Thai soup known for its distinctive blend of bold, tangy, and spicy flavors.
It is a beloved dish in Thai cuisine and is celebrated for its ability to awaken the palate with its vibrant and aromatic profile.

Tom Yum Soup-Prawns Ingrdients

  • 1 pound large prawns, peeled and deveined
  • 4 cups chicken or vegetable broth
  • 2-3 stalks lemongrass, cut into 2-inch pieces and smashed
  • 3-4 slices galangal or ginger
  • 3-4 kaffir lime leaves, torn into pieces
  • 2-3 Thai bird's eye chilies, smashed (adjust to your spice preference)
  • 200g (about 1 cup) mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 medium tomato, cut into wedges
  • 1 small onion, sliced
  • 2-3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons fish sauce
  • 1-2 tablespoons lime juice (adjust to taste)
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro leaves for garnish

How to make:

  1. In a large pot, bring the chicken or vegetable broth to a boil over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the lemongrass, galangal or ginger, kaffir lime leaves, and Thai bird's eye chilies to the boiling broth. Let it simmer for about 5-10 minutes to infuse the flavors.
  3. Add the sliced mushrooms, tomato wedges, and sliced onion to the pot. Simmer for another 5 minutes until the vegetables are slightly tender.
  4. Stir in the minced garlic and cook for an additional 1-2 minutes.
  5. Add the prawns (shrimp) to the pot and cook for about 3-5 minutes until they turn pink and opaque.
  6. Season the soup with fish sauce, lime juice, sugar, and salt to taste. Adjust the levels of fish sauce and lime juice according to your preferences for saltiness and tanginess.
  7. Remove the soup from heat and discard the lemongrass, galangal, kaffir lime leaves, and chilies if you prefer a milder flavor. Leave them in if you want it spicier and more aromatic.
  8. Serve hot, garnished with fresh cilantro leaves.

Enjoy your homemade Tom Yum Soup with Prawns! It's a delightful combination of sour, spicy, and savory flavors.
Adjust the level of spiciness and sourness to suit your taste.
