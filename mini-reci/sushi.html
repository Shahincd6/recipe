Rainbow Sushi Roll recipe

These are the basic ingredients youâ€™ll need to make this Rainbow Sushi Roll recipe at home:

  • Sushi rice (2 cups)
  • Nori sheets (seaweed sheets)
  • Sashimi-grade fish (such as tuna, salmon, and yellowtail)
  • Avocado
  • Cucumber
  • Carrot
  • Bell peppers (red, orange, yellow, or green)
  • Pickled ginger
  • Wasabi
  • Soy sauce
  • Sesame seeds (optional)
  • Bamboo sushi rolling mat
  • Plastic wrap

How to make Rainbow Sushi Roll

  1. Prepare Sushi Rice:
    • Rinse sushi rice under cold water until the water runs clear.
    • Cook the rice according to the package instructions.
    • Once cooked, season the rice with sushi vinegar or a mixture of rice vinegar, sugar, and salt. Allow it to cool to room temperature.
  2. Prepare Ingredients:
    • Slice the sashimi-grade fish into thin strips.
    • Cut the avocado, cucumber, carrot, and bell peppers into thin strips or julienne.
  3. Rolling the Sushi:
    • Place a bamboo sushi rolling mat on a clean surface and cover it with plastic wrap.
    • Lay a sheet of nori, shiny side down, on the plastic-covered sushi mat.
    • Wet your hands to prevent the rice from sticking, then grab a handful of sushi rice and spread it evenly over the nori sheet, leaving a small border at the top.
    • Lay strips of fish and vegetables horizontally across the center of the rice.
  4. Rolling the Rainbow Roll:
    • Using the bamboo mat, carefully lift the edge of the nori closest to you and start rolling it over the filling.
    • Use the mat to shape and tighten the roll as you go.
    • When you reach the exposed border of nori, wet it slightly and press to seal the roll.
  5. Cutting and Serving:
    • With a sharp knife dipped in water, cut the Rainbow Sushi Roll into bite-sized pieces.
    • Arrange the sushi pieces on a serving platter.
  6. Garnishing:
    • To make it resemble a rainbow, arrange thin strips of bell peppers on top of the sushi pieces, one color at a time.
    • Optionally, sprinkle sesame seeds over the top for added texture.
  7. Serve:
    • Serve your Rainbow Sushi Roll with pickled ginger, wasabi, and soy sauce on the side.

Enjoy your homemade Rainbow Sushi Roll! Feel free to get creative with the fish and vegetables you use to create your rainbow effect.
